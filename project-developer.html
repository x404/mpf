Dear Project Developer!

MyPowerFarm is a platform offering innovative way of financing renewable energy projects by using the mechanism of PowerUnits available for the purchase by retail investors.

We can offer you four possible ways of cooperation:

1

Project Developer does not invest in the construction of a Power Farm, his/her contribution ends with provision of the documents of a shovel-ready Project. This way, he/she gets 10% share in the Project financed by selling the Power Units to investors.

2

Project Developer contributes with documents showing shovel-ready status of the Project and also partly invests in the Project together with other investors via My Power Farm. His/Her share in the Project will depend on his/her investment value out of total.

3

Project Developer sells the Project rights at the shovel-ready stage to My Power Farm.

4

My Power Farm can be used to buy-out completed Projects at the operational stage.
