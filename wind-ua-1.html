Project description: MPF Wind UA 1 project is a Ukrainian project, with site on coast of the lake Sivash. The site is flat and grass covered, which facilitates construction works, and the surface roughness is accordingly low, reducing turbulence and wind fluctuations resulting in low stress on the wind turbines.

MPF Wind UA 1 is the second stage of an existing wind farm, consisting of two 600kW and sixteen 110kW turbines. GeoNet, has been performing a wind measurement campaign during 2 years using 100m met mast with wind measurement equipment provided by ENRGY. The wind report showed the result of mean wind speed 7,4m/s.

The substation is located in less than 1km distance and allows to connect 12M’s. For this porpoise there has been done wind measurements, environmental assessment, obtained land lease, grid connection rights and building permit.

Wind studies has been performed by Danish company “KenTec”, and showed that by using six 2MW Vestas V80 with 78m HH the capacity factor reaches 34,1%. It is going to be used refurbished wind turbines, completed by a well known wind turbine service provider.

The feed-in-tariff in Ukraine is enforced by the Law of Ukraine on Electric Energy Industry with Regards to Stimulation of Energy Generation from Alternative Energy Sources.

This project will reduce the CO2 emissions for 16.372 tons. It's generation will allow supply of electricity to 1328 households over a year.

Project now is in final development stage, precisely, on the stage of obtaining building permission.

The construction is scheduled for Q3 2017.
